Features

Battle, collect, craft, and grow stronger

Battle Stages

Survive waves of enemies, collect loot, and clear stages to unlock new areas with tougher monsters and better rewards.

Idle Battles

Each character class can save a run and idle it independently. Your battles are simulated live, earning different drops each time even while you're away.

Crafting & Forging

Use enemy drops and raw materials to craft useful items at the forge. Discover new recipes as you progress and spend what you craft on upgrades.

Apothecary & Garden

Tend your garden to grow ingredients like Hearthberries and Hot Peppers. Brew potions for permanent stat boosts that last forever.

Pets

Discover and raise companion pets that fight alongside you. Each pet has unique abilities like slowing, poisoning, or healing.

Upgrades

Spend loot to permanently upgrade your weapons, abilities, buildings, and more. Each class has different upgrades that persist between runs.

Player Classes

Three classes, each with unique abilities

Knight

Tank / Melee

Tough and armored. Wields a sword with abilities like Charge, Shield Wall, and Shockwave to control the battlefield.

Ranger

Ranged / Agile

Fast and deadly from afar. Fires piercing arrows with Sprint and Barrage abilities for hit-and-run tactics.

Miner

Passive / Melee

Slow and not the strongest, but well armored. Killing enemies spawns ores, letting you gather materials that other classes cannot obtain.
