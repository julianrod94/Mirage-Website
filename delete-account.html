Delete Account

Last updated: March 8, 2026

How to Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your Mirage account and all associated data, send an email to the developer contact email listed on the Mirage Google Play Store page. Please include the email address associated with your Google Sign-In so we can locate your account.

Your account and all associated data will be deleted and you will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed from our servers:

  • Your Google email address and display name
  • Your user ID and authentication tokens
  • Your cloud save data (game progress, inventory, upgrades, and all other saved game state)
  • Your cloud save history (up to 30 days of backup saves)

What Data Is Kept

No data is retained after account deletion. All data associated with your account is permanently deleted.

Your local save file on your device is not affected by account deletion. You can continue playing offline, but cloud sync will no longer be available until you sign in again (which creates a new account).

Deletion Timeline

Account deletion is processed within 7 days of your request. All associated data is permanently removed from our servers once the process is complete.

Contact

If you have trouble deleting your account or have questions, please contact us through the Google Play Store listing for Mirage.
